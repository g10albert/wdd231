Site Name: Camara Santiago Digital Site Purpose: To serve as the primary digital hub for Santiago’s business community, facilitating economic growth by providing resources for local entrepreneurs, promoting industrial investment, and simplifying administrative tasks (like business registration) for members. Target Market: - Local Small-to-Medium Enterprises (SMEs): Business owners in Santiago looking for networking and growth. - Industrial Leaders: Stakeholders in the Santiago Free Zone (Zona Franca) and manufacturing sectors. - International Investors: People looking to start or move a business to the Cibao region Site Goals: - Modernization: Replace manual processes with digital forms for membership and renewals. - Information Hub: Provide real-time updates on local economic laws, trade events, and workshops. - Visibility: Showcase a searchable directory of Santiago-based businesses to encourage local "B2B" trade. User Personas - Startup Founder: Alejandro wants to network and find mentors in Santiago. He doesn't know where to find legal requirements for a new business. - Indultrial Manager: Elena needs to stay updated on export regulations and Free Zone news. Current information is scattered across different PDFs. Scenarios - Scenario A: Alejandro has just started a small tech firm in Los Jardines. He visits the site to find a "Membership Benefits" page and signs up online. - Scenario B: An investor from Miami wants to know the tax benefits of the Santiago Free Zone. They use the site’s search bar to find the "Investment Guide." SEO Plan - Keywords: "Cámara de Comercio Santiago," "Negocios en Santiago RD," "Invertir en el Cibao," "Directorio comercial Santiago." - Strategy: Use localized metadata - Content: Create a blog called "Pulso del Cibao" featuring success stories of local businesses to rank for local industry keywords.
